About Me

I started to code my senior year of college. Through Python, I began to frequent a world governed by the marriage between logic and artistry; a world that, up until that point in my life, I had only been able to visit through music. After spending a year working in Revenue Operations at Toast, Inc., I decided to transition full-time into web development. I spent much of my time at Toast writing Salesforce integrations in Python that automated rote tasks that other members of the operations team were performing. I just completed App Academy's 6-month intensive bootcamp and am looking forward to my next opportunity. Check out my resume.
